The world is called Furvista

There are 3 continents, Humeria, Dracalia, and Vulperia

The continent of Dracalia has been closed off for hundred of years

The continent of Humeria is the smallest continent, and is mainly controlled by humans

The largest continent, Vulperia, is home to most of the anthro species of the world.

Vulperia is split into 4 kingdoms, Sioneran, Lupindas, Ollabun, Whisperin

The capital of Sioneran is the City of Furlyria

Vulperia has magic based technology.
